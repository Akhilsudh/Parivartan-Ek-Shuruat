Tablets and smartphones very many have replaced the personal computers in their classic version with keyboard, mouse, monitor and the system unit. Mobile devices are becoming more comfortable, functional and productive. But to give up entirely the some accessories such as a keyboard is still not yet possible, as quickly gain business documents on the touch screen is not as convenient. A new gadget called Zagg was recently presented a group of enthusiastic developers. This device is a compact, portable keyboard for iPhone, iPad and other devices, which has dimensions of 12 inches when folded. In the unfolded form of the device is almost a full keyboard (85% of the standard). Irreplaceable thing, when you want to respond to emails and edit documents in the hiking conditions.
