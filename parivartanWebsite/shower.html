Musical Shower

Shower With Music

Sometimes, singing in the shower unaccompanied is just not enough. This is where the Moxie Showerhead and Wireless Speaker comes in. From Kohler, the Moxie showerhead gives you full spray coverage with 60 angled nozzles and provides up to 7 hours of uninterrupted music, news, and other audio material using bluetooth technology. Moxie is also environmentally friendly, with an option for 2.0 gpm or 2.5 gpm spray, and its silicon surface makes it easy to clean. It can be synced with your bluetooth-linked device from up to 32 feet away. And don't be too worried about keeping the speaker charged, either. It pops in and out easily and its built-in lithium-ion battery is recharged with a USB cable.
