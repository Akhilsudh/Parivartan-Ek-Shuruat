Tape Record to mp3 Converter

This gadget converts audio tape cassettes in to MP3 format files and stores these straight on to the iPhone or even iPod-touch. Using its totally free application, conversion to MP3 fornat is as easy as inserting the cassette, docking the iPhone or iPod-touch, and pushing play. This allows many iPhones and iPod devices, and standard or chrome cassette tapes. Tape could be listened utilizing the headphones jack port or through connecting the audio output into a audio system for scanning forward or reverse to pick tracks for conversions.
